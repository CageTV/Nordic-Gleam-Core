# Nordic Gleam Core

**Nordic Gleam Core** reimagines Skyrim through a **lore-friendly, survival-focused lens**.  
Built for players who crave **challenge and immersion**, this list enhances the vanilla experience with cutting-edge PBR visuals, upscaled assets, and minimal landscape edits—preserving Skyrim’s soul while bringing it into a new generation.

Expect tougher hunting, harsher survival conditions, and subtle world changes that make everything feel **fresh yet familiar**.

---

## Features

- **Lore-Friendly Visuals** – Upgraded textures, realistic materials, and PBR lighting while maintaining the vanilla aesthetic.  
- **Survival Overhaul** – Manage hunger, warmth, exhaustion, and harsh weather.  
- **Combat Evolution** – Balanced, timing-based MCO combat that rewards skill and awareness.  
- **Immersive World** – Small environmental edits and wildlife tweaks create a rebalanced sense of wilderness.  
- **Performance Focused** – Carefully tuned for smooth gameplay on modern hardware.

---

## Things to Do

Once installed and running, here are some recommended steps and ideas:

- Visit **Riverwood** to gather supplies and test your survival loadout.  
- Travel north to **Whiterun** and check out vendors for early gear upgrades.  
- Experiment with hunting and cooking systems — survival is key.  
- Explore side paths and dungeons; many visuals and encounters have been subtly enhanced.  
- Use your MCO dodge and blocking mechanics effectively — timing matters!  
- Keep an eye on warmth and weather conditions before venturing into the north.

## Installation

1. Install **Skyrim Anniversary Edition** (version **1.6.1170**).  
2. Download and install **Wabbajack** from [wabbajack.org](https://www.wabbajack.org/).  
3. Download this repository’s `.wabbajack` file and select an installation folder outside `Program Files`.  
4. Place the manual downloads listed above into your Wabbajack `downloads` folder.  
5. Run Wabbajack and complete the installation.  

Once done, use **Mod Organizer 2** to launch Skyrim through the provided executable.

---

## Notes

- Do **not** manually add or remove mods unless you understand the load order.  
- Best played with **survival mode enabled**.  
- For performance tuning, adjust ENB presets and resolution scaling.
